01 Debut Album
album art — coming soon

Original Work · In Production

Written from
the inside out.

My debut album is a deeply personal body of work — written, composed and produced entirely by me. It draws from my life as a young woman in her 20s: navigating relationships, moving to a foreign country, tough situations, and expressing my true feelings about all of it.

For years, there are many things I have wanted to say out loud. I realized I have a voice, and this is the first time I've decided to harness it's full power. No filter. No compromise. Just my voice, my instruments, and everything I had to say, in one album.
